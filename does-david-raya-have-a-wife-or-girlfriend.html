David Raya Martín was born on September 15, 1995 in Spain. He is a proficient Spanish footballer specializing as a goalkeeper. He is a valuable member of the Premier League squad Brentford and is also a key player for the Spain national team. Raya commenced his professional journey in English football with Blackburn Rovers.

His breakthrough moment arrived when he played a pivotal role in the team’s promotion from League One in 2018. Subsequently, in 2019, Raya made a transfer to Brentford, a Championship club, and contributed significantly to their elevation to the Premier League in 2021. In the year 2022, he proudly represented Spain on the international stage, marking his debut.

His early sporting days were rooted in his homeland, Spain. He honed his goalkeeping skills while simultaneously participating as an outfield player in futsal. Progressing in his career, he engaged in youth football at UE Cornellà, before venturing to England in July 2012 to embark on a scholarship with Blackburn Rovers.

Notably, an agreement between the two clubs was struck, allowing UE Cornellà players to undergo trials at Blackburn Rovers following Hugo Fernández’s transfer to Ewood Park. Raya’s development continued through the club’s academy system, culminating in the signing of his professional contract on February 26, 2014.

During the first half of the 2014–15 season, he gained invaluable senior experience, spending four months on loan at Conference Premier club Southport, amassing 24 appearances. Upon his return to Ewood Park, he contributed with two late-season Championship appearances and solidified his commitment by signing a fresh three-year contract in April 2015.

Although his appearances were limited to just 13 across the 2015–16 and 2016–17 seasons, Raya served as the second-choice goalkeeper for Rovers, frequently featuring in the matchday squad behind Jason Steele.

The conclusion of the 2016–17 season saw Rovers facing relegation to League One, a circumstance that propelled Raya into the role of the club’s primary goalkeeper. During the 2017–18 season, he showcased his talent in 47 appearances, playing a pivotal part in the club’s swift return to the Championship through automatic promotion.

His prowess continued into the 2018–19 season, amassing 46 appearances during a period of consolidation in the Championship. Raya’s association with Rovers concluded in July 2019, having contributed 108 appearances to the club’s legacy.
