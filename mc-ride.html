Biography

MC Ride was born in Sacramento, CA on May 10, 1978. Most well known for fronting the California rap group Death Grips, the artist known as MC Ride is recognized for his mysterious, dark lyrics and for his in-your-face manner of rapping. His recordings with Death Grips include Exmilitary (2011), No Love Deep Web (2012), and The Powers That B (2015). He and drummer Zach Hill are both known for their work with Death Grips. After briefly studying at Hampton University in Virginia, he launched the hip-hop project Fyre and began rapping under the stage name of MxlPlx.
