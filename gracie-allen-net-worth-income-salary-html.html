She was born Grace Ethel Cecile Rosalie “Gracie” Allen on July 26, 1895, in New York City, and died on August 27, 1964, in Los Angeles. She was an American comedienne who became globally renowned as the wacky companion and comedic foil of husband George Burns. Gracie Allen has been recognised with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame, located at 6672 Hollywood Boulevard, in recognition of her services to the television industry. She and her husband, George Burns, are also both members of the Television Hall of Fame, having been inducted into the organisation together in 1988.
