Who is Larry Hoover?

Larry Hoover is a former American gang leader and co-founder of the Gangster Disciples street gang in Chicago. At the ADX Florence prison in Florence, Colorado, Hoover is presently serving six life sentences. He received a life sentence in 1997 after being given a 200-year term for a murder that occurred in 1973.

Larry Hoover bio

He was born in the US on November 30, 1950, in Jackson, Mississippi. His family relocated to Chicago, Illinois, when he was a young child. When he was 12 years old, he started participating in criminal acts with his buddies, such as muggings and robberies. He and his gangmates came up with the name “Supreme Gangsters,” and he eventually rose to the position of group leader.
