One of the disadvantages of working in the entertainment industry is the needless speculation about people’s private lives, sexual preferences, and even death hoaxes.

The same incident happened to legendary actor and comedian Reginald VelJohnson.

The Family Matters actor VelJohnson, best remembered for his portrayal of Carl Winslow, has regularly made news for his LGBT orientation. He is thought to be gay, and there is good reason for this belief.

Television Son of VelJohnson Supported Father’s Sexuality Secrecy

How convincingly an actor can depict a character—to the point that viewers lose track that they are watching a performance—determines how valuable they are.

This also applies to VelJohnson. His character Winslow is adored by viewers all across the world. Even when he has nothing to do with an interview, people try to find a way to learn more about the well-known TV parent.

When VelJohnson’s TV son, Darius McCrary, sat down to discuss the program with the Comedy Hype, he was questioned about if VelJohnson’s sexual orientation had ever come up when he was on Family Matters.

The actor declared categorically that he has never understood why people are offended by someone’s sexual orientation or personal preferences.

Additionally, McCrary said he was not a fan of the media dictating how people are portrayed and what the audience thinks.

The rumors stopped after Avery got married. Both performers refuted any reports and choose to remain silent despite the fact that some people think it was an open secret and others question their relationship.
