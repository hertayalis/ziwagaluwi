Jennifer Lopez Shows Us How the Mob Wife Aesthetic Is Done

July 25, 2024
blog

Everyone and their mother is joining in on the viral “mob wife” aesthetic, with stars like Khloé Kardashian and Emily Ratajkowski pulling out their best fur, gold jewelry, and logo-emblazoned accessories—the current go-to uniform for fashion TikTokers.

Cue Jennifer Lopez, who showed us how it was done with a casual ensemble she wore Thursday while walking the streets of New York City. Jenny From the Block was spotted out and about in a screen-print tee and white puddle pants from Les Tien, which she tucked into thick snowy boots that had rows of Velcro straps going down the front and beige detailing all around the outside.

She kept the pearly color scheme going with a white baseball cap, and she embraced the mob wife vibe with large golden hoop earrings and fluffy vanilla fur jacket (which appears to be the same one she wore during Paris Haute Couture Week).

Backgrid//BACKGRID

Then, Lopez added pops of color with her two bags, one of which was a teeny pistachio crossbody with a front flap closure and decorative studs. She also carried a duffle bag from Gucci that featured the brand’s signature webbed pattern with a double-G logo, as well as red and green stripes down the center. In a very J.Lo move, she had the duffle monogrammed with her nickname, which you can see on the side.

As we’ve seen in the past, the Marry Me star enjoys getting her name embroidered onto her clothes. Let’s not forget back in May 2023, Lopez sported a cream knit cardigan that had her initials stitched on one of the cuffs in red. How very Carrie Bradshaw of her.
