Kim Kardashian Just Teased the Family Christmas Card With Sweet Pics of Saint and North

September 06, 2024
blog

Kim Kardashian just shared the first two snaps from the Kardashian family's annual Christmas card, and they are cuter than ever. Released in advent calendar format, with a new photo to be shared each day, the first two feature her children, North and Saint. Kim revealed on Snapchat that this year's family photo was shot by Eli Linnetz, and is called "25 Days of Christmas."

Day 2 is a close-up of North, next to mom Kim, and aunt Kourtney Kardashian:

Kim also shared a photos of husband Kanye West with Chris Rock, and Eddie Murphy:

Comedian Amanda Seales tweeted that she "fulfilled a dream and opened up for @chrisrock in LA... AND met Eddie Murphy." The evening was obviously a star-studded affair, considering that Kim and Kanye were in the audience too.

As for the Kardashian family Christmas card, Kim told Entertainment Tonight that organizing the photoshoot, with photographer Eli Linnetz, proved to be a bit of a logistical nightmare. She said:

"I'll never do it again. Just to wrangle ever single family member was so exhausting. I thought it would be really easy. It was really tough. But it'll be a good one this year, I think our best yet."

While Kim doesn't want to step into momager Kris Jenner's shoes just yet, it's clear that she is more than capable of helping to create an iconic Christmas card photo. And as this year's shoot takes the form of an advent calendar, we have so much more to look forward to in days to come.
