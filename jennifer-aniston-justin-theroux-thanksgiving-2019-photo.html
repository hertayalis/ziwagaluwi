Jennifer Aniston &amp; Justin Theroux Photographed Together on Thanksgiving 2019

May 18, 2024
blog

When it comes to staying on good terms with exes, Jennifer Aniston is a literal professional.

Having invited her first husband, Brad Pitt, to her 50th birthday party, Aniston proved that she's still great friends with her recent ex, Justin Theroux, over Thanksgiving 2019. In a selfie shared by Theroux on Instagram, the actor can be seen posing with a huge group of people at Aniston's "Friendsgiving" party, which she holds every year on the day before Thanksgiving.

Also in the photo are Jason Bateman, Jimmy Kimmel, Will Arnett, and Courteney Cox.

Instagram

In September 2018, Theroux opened up about his split from Aniston in an interview with The New York Times. He explained, "The good news is that was probably the most—I’m choosing my words really carefully—it was kind of the most gentle separation, in that there was no animosity."

Meanwhile, Aniston also shared some snaps from her pre-Thanksgiving party on Instagram. The star of The Morning Show revealed that she'd prepared an extra special snack for talk show host, Jimmy Kimmel. Per Aniston, "Ok, @jimmykimmel... here are your f*%king Friendsgiving enchiladas."

Understandably, Kimmel was pretty excited about seeing enchiladas at the party, having previously requested that Aniston make something other than turkey this year.
