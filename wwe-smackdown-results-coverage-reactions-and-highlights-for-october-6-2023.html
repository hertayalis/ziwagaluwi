WWE SmackDown Results Coverage, Reactions and Highlights For October 6, 2023

August 12, 2024
blog

Coming to you live on Friday Nights, it’s WWE SmackDown and Ringside News has got you covered with live results.

Start time for SmackDown is 8:00 PM EST Match-by-match highlights and results from the show will take place on this very page. Feel free to leave any reactions in the comment section below.

SmackDown tonight is set to feature special appearances from Cody Rhodes, Jey Uso and The Judgment Day ahead of the Fastlane event.

SmackDown also has the following matches scheduled for tonight:

Austi Theory vs Dragon Lee

Charlotte Flair & Asuka vs Damage CTRL

Rey Mysterio vs Bobby Lashley

It should be interesting to see how things play out on tonight’s show.

SmackDown kicks off live with LA Knight making his way out to the ring in front of a crowd very happy to see him. LA Knight grabs the mic as the crowd chants his name. Right as LA Knight is ready to talk to us he is cut off by Paul Heyman walking out with Solo Sikoa and Jimmy Uso. Heyman says that LA Knight is going to be the next big mega star in WWE. Heyman says LA Knight has all the momentum in the world and has earned Heyman’s respect and admiration. Heyman says that now that he’s earned Heyman’s attention, the Bloodline is going to have to do something about it.

LA Knight says Heyman came out and told him everything great about him but LA Knight didn’t buy any of it. Jimmy Uso cuts LA Knight off. Jimmy tells LA Knight he won’t make it to Fastlane and Jimmy and Sikoa begin to climb up to the ring when John Cena’s music hits and Cena runs out and into the ring to even the odds. Jimmy and Sikoa follow Heyman’s orders and abandon the ringside area. LA Knight says they didn’t have permission from their chief to come out tonight. LA Knight says he’s thinking about tonight and tonight LA Knight wants Jimmy Uso one on one. Jimmy Uso accepts the challenge as Heyman looks absolutely bewildered at everything that just happened.

The Judgment Day are seen walking in the arena from the parking lot as they have arrived to SmackDown.

Charlotte Flair makes her way out to the ring for our upcoming tag team match.

*Commercial break*

Charlotte Flair & Asuka vs Damage CTRL

Our opening tag team match begins with Sky avoiding a big boot from Charlotte and hitting a head-scissors take down. Charlotte hits a back breaker and covers for a two count. Asuka tags in the match and Asuka hits Sky with a series of knife-edge chops. Sky ducks under a kick and gets a roll up for a two count. Asuka goes for a corner drop kick but Bayley shoves Sky out of the corner and takes the kick in sky’s place. Bayley gets in the ring and Asuka hits a double drop kick from the top rope to take out Sky and Bayley.

*Commercial break*

The match continues live with Bayley having Asuka in a chin lock on the mat. Bayley picks Asuka up and hits a snap suplex, Bayley then covers for a two count. Bayley tags Sky back in the match. Sky locks in a body-scissors and bites down on Asuka’s face as the ref is out of position to see it. Bayley tags back in and slams Asuka into the heel team’s corner. Bayley takes a cheap shot to knock Charlotte off the apron and then Sky tags back in and Sky and Bayley hit a double power slam, Sky goes for a cover but Charlotte runs in to pull Sky off. Asuka crawls to the corner to make the hot tag as Bayley is also tagged in. Charlotte hits a flying cross body and follows with a series of chops. Charlotte goes for her signature lariat but Bayley counters with a forearm strike. Charlotte fights out of the corner and hits the lariat this time and covers for two. Bayley irish whips Charlotte to the corner and Asuka makes the blind tag. Asuka places Bayley on the top rope and Charlotte tags back in. Charlotte and Asuka go for a double superplex but Sky runs in to make it a full tower of doom spot. Charlotte hits a big boot and covers but Sky breaks it up with a meteora. Bayley ducks out of a big boot which causes Charlotte to hit Asuka. Bayley goes for a figure 8 on Charlotte but Charlotte breaks free and launches Bayley into Sky on the ring apron. Charlotte hits the natural selection and covers Bayley for three.

Winners: Charlotte Flair & Asuka

Backstage Jimmy Uso and Solo Sikoa walk into the locker room to see The Judgment Day sitting there as tensions rise between the two sides.

*Commercial break*

Back live with the Bloodline and Judgment Day staring intensely at each other as I choose to believe they did the entire commercial break. Rhea Ripley calls for everyone else to leave as she wishes to speak with Paul Heyman alone.

Rey Mysterio makes his way out to the ring along with the rest of LWO for his upcoming non-title match against Bobby Lashley.

*Commercial break*

Rey Mysterio vs Bobby Lashley

The match begins with a lock up. Lashley powers Mysterio to the corner. Lashley hits Mysterio with a big shoulder tackle and then hits a corner spear. Lashley goes for a power slam but Mysterio lands on his feet and hits a kick to Lashley followed by a springboard moonsault. Lashley rolls to the apron and Mysterio goes for a 619 but Lashley rolls out to the floor.

*Commercial break*

The match continues live with Lashley having Mysterio locked in a bear hug. Mysterio is able to break free but is Irish whipped into the corner and Lashley locks in another bear hug. Mysterio breaks free with a series of headbutts but is caught yet again by Lashley in a bear hug. Mysterio once again breaks free and climbd to the top rope. Lashley grabs Mysterio on the top rope and goes for a corner spear but Mysterio avoids it to send Lashley crashing to the ring post. Mysterio hits a seated senton and Mysterio hits a tornado DDT. Lashley goes for a delayed vertical suplex but Mysterio breaks free and knocks Lashley to the ropes, Mysterio goes for the 619 but Lashley catches Mysterio’s legs and Lashley knocks Mysterio out of the ring with a forearm strike. Lashley throws Mysterio to the barricade and back in the ring. Lashley also throws Santos Escobar over the barricade for good measure. Mysterio goes for a dive outside but is caught by Lashley. Mysterio is able to send Lashley into the ring post, back in the ring Mysterio hits the 619 and climbs to the top rope but Montez Ford causes a distraction that eventually leads to a brawl between the LWO and Street Profits. Mysterio then hits a senton to take out both Ford and Dawkins. Mysterio gets back into the ring right into a spear by Lashley. Lashley covers Mysterio for three.

Winner: Bobby Lashley

We go backstage where we see Paul Heyman and Rhea Ripley sitting together. Ripley tells Heyman to make the wise decision, saying that they are stronger together. Heyman says he likes the idea, and he is going to make a call to get it authorized. Heyman goes to call but Ripley grabs the phone to stop Heyman, Ripley tells Heyman it’s authorized and Ripley tells Heyman to acknowledge her. Heyman tells Ripley she is in the wrong locker room to say something like that. Ripley tells Heyman he will acknowledge her or they will find out who’s stronger, The Judgment Day or the Bloodline, Ripley then tells Heyman he is authorized to make his phone call and Ripley leaves.

Austin Theory makes his way out to the ring as he is set to go one on one with Dragon Lee up next.

*Commercial break*

Austin Theory vs Dragon Lee

Before the bell rings Theory grabs the mic and says they are all in the presence of glory. Theory says that he is the 150 million hit man and as soon as the bell rings Dragon Lee will experience Austin Theory live.

The bell ringa and the match gets underway with Lee hitting a big huricanrana. The match in interrupted by Grayson Waller making his way out to the ring. Off this distraction Theory is able to attack Lee from behind. Lee hits a drop kick and covers for two. Lee hits a snap German suplex and Theory rolls out of the ring. Waller gets on the ring apron and Theory gets back in the ring and hit a drop kick.

*Commercial break*

The match continues live with Theory rolling into the ring for Lee to hit a super kick. Lee hits a drop kick in the corner. Lee counters a lariat and hits a Liger bomb and covers for a two count. Lee climbs to the top rope but Theory knocks Lee off the ropes after Waller causes yet another distraction. Waller drags Theory out of the ring and Lee responds by hitting a dive over the top to take out Waller and Theory. The ref checks on Theory and Waller slams Lee’s head off the ring apron. Cameron Grimes runs out to take out Waller, this distraction allows Lee to get a roll up for the three count.

Winner: Dragon Lee

*Commercial break*

Back live with Rey Mysterio meeting with Santos Escobar and Zelina Vega backstage. Mysterio says that Cruz Del Toro and Joaquin Wilde won’t be cleared to compete tomorrow night. As LWO worries about their six man tag match tomorrow at Fastlane Mysterio says he will make a call to get things taken care of for the match tomorrow.

*Commercial break*

Jimmy Uso vs LA Knight

Our main event begins with a lock up. Jimmy shoves LA Knight to the corner and goes for a right hand strike but LA Knight dodges and locks Jimmy in a side headlock. Jimmy hits a shoulder tackle and after Jimmy poses for the crowd LA Knight hits a shoulder tackle of his own and follows with a power slam. LA Knight goes for the BFT but Jimmy escapes and rolls out of the ring. Jimmy hits an enziguri to knock LA Knight out of the ring and Jimmy then hits a super kick.

*Commercial break*

Our main event continues live with LA Knight hitting a sunset flip into a powerbomb. LA Knight hits a side Russian leg sweep and then follows with a series of stomps in the corner and a big corner splash. LA Knight hits an elbow srop and Solo Sikoa runs into the ring to attack LA Knight and cause the DQ finish

Winner by DQ: LA Knight

After the bell John Cena runs out to make the save. Knight and Cena clear the ring and then The Judgment Day make their way out to the ring. Paul Heyman and Rhea Ripley shake hands as Heyman tells Ripley it’s authorized. The Judgment Day and Bloodline all get on the apron when Jey Uso’s music hits. Jey runs put to the ring to join LA Knight and Cena. Following this Cody Rhodes then runs out to join and fully start the brawl. Cody hits a tope suicida which is followed by Jey hitting a dive over the top rope. Sikoa hits Cena with a super kick and LA Knight runs in to knock Sikoa out of the ring. JD McDonagh runs in and LA Knight hits the BFT, then Jey hits JD with a super kick and then Cena hits an AA to JD and then for good measure Cody hits a cross Rhodes.

We get one more run down of the card for Fastlane as Cody Rhodes’ theme plays in the background as SmackDown goes off the air.
