Ever wondered what life is like for WWE stars out of the ring? What's their day-to-day personal life like? Then make sure you tune in to 'Miz & Mrs' featuring WWE superstars Mike and Maryse Mizanin. The show follows the two WWE stars as they juggle between their stardom and new role as parents.

Release date

Season 2  of 'Miz & Mrs' is all set to premiere on Wednesday, January 29, only on USA Network. Following the premiere, you can catch new episodes of the show every Wednesday following the premiere only on USA Network.

Plot

Season 2 will see Mike & Maryse returning to California to raise their two little children. The show chronicles the lives of new parents, Mike and Maryse, as they embrace parenthood. The network website describes the show as, "Miz & Mrs is a docuseries chronicling the lives of WWE Superstars and married couple Mike and Maryse. The six-episode, half-hour series will feature the pair, known for being larger than life both in and out of the ring, as they become parents for the first time."

Cast

Mike 'The Miz' Mizanin

Mike began his journey in the glitzy world of reality television with his stint on the popular reality show, 'The Real World: Back to New York'. After the end of the show, he didn't stop there, he used his new-found fame to launch a career in WWE. He soon became a well-known name in the WWE circles and landed several roles in films and TV series. Which eventually got him a plum opportunity of hosting his own talk show called 'The Miz TV'. 

Maryse Mizanin

The stunning beauty has quite a few impressive feathers in her hat, from being the first two-time Divas Champion to the longest-tenured Divas Champion in history. Apart from being a very successful wrestler, she is also a great talent manager. She has appeared on shows like Total Divas'. 'Total Bellas' and 'WAGS'  in the past.

Creators

The show is executive produced by Mike and Maryse, along with Farnaz Farjam-Chazan, Gil Goldschein, Russell Jay and Kevin Dunn.
