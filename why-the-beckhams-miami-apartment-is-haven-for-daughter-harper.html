Why Victoria and David Beckham's $24m Miami apartment is haven for daughter Harper

August 11, 2024
blog

Victoria and David Beckham have the most impressive property portfolio with a main base in London worth a whopping £31million, a converted farmhouse in the Cotswolds and a jaw-dropping Miami apartment.

The latter may be the least frequented of the three, but it certainly is the most glam! Their $24million Miami penthouse is located within the One Hundred Museum building, a luxurious block based on a seven-star hotel.

Facilities were a priority when the building was designed, and many of the features are perfect for their daughter Harper Seven.

The 11-year-old is a big fan of swimming, as we've seen on Victoria's Instagram feed on various occasions and each of the penthouse suits has its own private pool – plus there's an infinity pool for communal use.

We've seen Harper enjoy trying on outfits with her designer mother, and we're sure Victoria's impressive dressing room is the ideal place to play dress up.

The mother-daughter duo have previously enjoyed many spa dates together, and in their Miami home they don't even have to go out for a dose of rest and relaxation.

Other notable features include an acai and juice bar, and a sunbathing area. With all of these amenities, we don't think we'd ever leave.
