A clash of undefeated heavyweights will kick off the PPV portion of the Fury Vs Wilder card. Jared ‘Big Baby’ Anderson will take on Vladimir Tereshkin in an epic clash. Since turning pro at the age of 19, Tereshkin has maintained his unbeaten record. The Russian is unbeaten in 14 years. However, he knows Anderson will be one of his toughest fights to date. “My opponent is a strong fighter, but I’m prepared as well as I ever have been,” he confirmed. “He’s going to have to be ready for what I bring to the ring, just like I’m going to be.”

Jared ‘Big Baby’ Anderson will make his PPV debut in his 10th professional fight. Anderson first turned heads when he fought as an amateur. After going pro, he has caught the eyes of many promoters and secured a deal with Top Rank. He also sparred with Tyson ‘The Gypsy King’ Fury ahead of his second fight vs Deontay ‘The Bronze Bomber’ Wilder.

Now the American will fight on the undercard of his old sparring partner and look to put the world on notice. A win against the experienced Tereshkin would immediately turn heads in the heavyweight division, making him a worthy challenger in a star-studded division.

Ahead of their fight, let’s take a look at the stats of the two heavyweights. Age, height, weight, boxing record, etc.
